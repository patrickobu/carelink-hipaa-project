## BUSINESS ASSOCIATE AGREEMENT

This Business Associate Agreement is entered into between:

**Covered Entity:** CareLink Health (referred to as "CE")

**Business Associate:** [Vendor Name] (referred to as "BA")

---

## 1. Definitions

**Protected Health Information (PHI):** Any individually identifiable
health information created, received, maintained, or transmitted by
the BA on behalf of the CE.

**Business Associate:** A person or entity that performs functions
or activities on behalf of a covered entity that involve the use
or disclosure of PHI.

---

## 2. Obligations of the Business Associate

The BA agrees to:

a) Not use or disclose PHI other than as permitted by this agreement
   or required by law

b) Use appropriate safeguards to prevent unauthorised use or
   disclosure of PHI

c) Report to the CE any use or disclosure of PHI not permitted by
   this agreement within 5 business days of discovery

d) Report any security incident or breach of unsecured PHI within
   60 days of discovery

e) Ensure any subcontractors that access PHI agree to the same
   restrictions and conditions

f) Make available PHI for patient access requests within 30 days

g) Make internal practices available to the Secretary of HHS
   for audit purposes

h) Return or destroy all PHI upon termination of this agreement

---

## 3. Permitted Uses and Disclosures

The BA may use or disclose PHI only to:

a) Perform the services described in the underlying services agreement

b) Manage and administer its own business operations

c) Provide data aggregation services relating to the CE's health
   care operations

d) Report violations of law to appropriate authorities

---

## 4. CE Obligations

The CE agrees to:

a) Notify the BA of any limitations in its Notice of Privacy Practices

b) Notify the BA of any changes in or revocation of patient consent

c) Not request the BA to use or disclose PHI in a way that would
   violate HIPAA

---

## 5. Term and Termination

This agreement begins on the date of signature and remains in effect
until terminated. Either party may terminate for material breach
with 30 days written notice.

Upon termination the BA shall return or destroy all PHI.
